The Problem

BARBER SHOPS ARE
STUCK IN 1995.

😤
You Wait. And Wait. And Wait.
Walk into any barber shop in India and you're met with plastic chairs, a TV playing old songs, and zero clarity on how long you'll be there.
📞
Booking? Just WhatsApp Them.
Most shops have no system. You call, they forget. You WhatsApp, they miss it. You just show up and hope for the best.
💸
Barbers Lose Money on Empty Slots
Last-minute cancellations and no-shows leave barbers with dead time and lost income they can't recover.
🤷
No Visibility. No Control.
Shop owners have no idea how many customers are coming today. No data. No planning. Just vibes.
BarberBook Fixes This.

We're bringing the same logic that Uber used for cabs and Zomato used for food — to your neighbourhood barber shop.

01
Real-time token system — see exactly who's ahead of you and when your turn is.
02
Pre-book your slot — choose your barber, choose your time, get a confirmation instantly.
03
Barber dashboard — barbers see their full day at a glance. No surprises.
04
Owner analytics — daily bookings, peak hours, revenue tracking — all in one screen.

Features

EVERYTHING A BARBER
SHOP NEEDS.

🎟️
Token Queue System
FCFS-based token assignment. Book online, get your number, know your exact slot before you leave home.
✂️
Choose Your Barber
Each barber has their own queue. Pick your favourite, see their availability, book directly with them.
⏱️
Live Wait Time
Real-time updates on the queue. Watch your token move up. Get notified when you're next.
📅
Easy Reschedule
Plans changed? Reschedule or cancel with a tap. Your slot goes back to the pool for someone else.
📊
Owner Dashboard
See daily bookings, peak hours, per-barber stats and revenue — all in a clean overview screen.
🔔
Smart Reminders
Push notifications remind customers 30 minutes before their slot. Fewer no-shows. More revenue.
How It Works

BOOK IN
4 STEPS.

1
Open the App
Find your nearest listed barber shop or scan their QR code at the entrance.
2
Pick a Slot
Choose your barber and a time that works. See real-time availability before you book.
3
Get Your Token
Instantly receive your token number and estimated time. Save it or screenshot it.
4
Walk In On Time
Show up at your slot. No waiting. Sit in the chair, get your cut, walk out.
